My Story

From Curious Beginner
to AI Creative

Not too long ago, I was exactly where you might be right now — scrolling past incredible AI-generated content and wondering, "Could I do that?" I was a creative at heart with no tech background, a full plate of responsibilities, and a deep desire to build something of my own.

I picked up my first AI tool out of curiosity. Within weeks, I was creating stunning photorealistic images, African-inspired lifestyle shoots, and digital products that people actually wanted to buy. That curiosity became a business — and a mission.

"I believe every woman, every creator, every entrepreneur deserves access to the tools that can transform their vision into reality. AI is that tool — and I'm here to show you how."

Today, I help beginners and busy moms build skills and income with AI. I create content for brands, teach practical AI tutorials, and sell digital products that make learning easier. My work is rooted in warmth, authenticity, and the African creative spirit.
